Halloween Mad-Lib

There once was a lonely man who lived in a house. One day, he heard a loud noise coming from the basement. He walked to the basement door, to see what the noise was. When he looked down the stairwell, he saw a(n) scary ghost. Frightened, the man ran away, calling for his teddy bear. The end.

Once upon a time, there were two happy spirits. One spirit looked to the other and said, "Have you seen my book?" "I haven't", the second spirit lied as he ran down the road. "Instead, why don't you take my red pen?" The first spirit took a moment to reply. Finally he said, "Okay, I will." The end.
