Why These Videos?

8 Out of 10 Cats

Over the years, I have developed a profound love for British game shows. Where American game shows are all about winning, a lot of the British game shows are just a bunch of comedians playing games on television. They often make me laugh and bring me enjoyment after a stressful day at work.

VFX Artists React

I enjoy watching movies, and having been a part of stage productions, I enjoy behind-the-scenes videos that show how movies are made. These videos are really fun because industry professionals give a look at some good and bad CGi, and provide insight as to what makes it good/bad. These videos provide a chance to look back at films I have seen in the past, and learn something new about the industry.

Critical Role

Critical Role combines my interest in voice actors and role playing games. I love playing games like Dungeons and Dragons, and seeing professional voice actors play D&D gives me inspiration and joy.

The Game Theorists

I often watch video essays and think pieces. The Game Theorists both delve deep into research to provide stimulating and provocative video essays, but they are all centered around video games. I have fun thinking about these crazy video game based theories.
